Measurement Framework

Review Cadence

KPIs are reviewed on the following schedule:

  • Daily/Weekly KPIs: Reviewed in weekly operations standup
  • Monthly KPIs: Reviewed in monthly strategy check-in with full team
  • Quarterly KPIs: Reviewed in quarterly board/advisory meeting with plan adjustment authority

Escalation Protocol

Condition Action Timeline
KPI trending 10%+ below target for 2+ measurement periods Root cause analysis and corrective action plan Within 1 week of identification
KPI trending 25%+ below target for 1+ measurement period Escalate to strategy owner; evaluate pillar adjustment Within 48 hours of identification
3+ KPIs simultaneously below target Full strategy review and potential plan pivot Emergency strategy session within 1 week
KPI exceeding target by 25%+ Evaluate acceleration opportunity; reallocate budget Next scheduled review
